**Project Music** is a high-performance, modern music player for Android, built from the ground up with **Jetpack Compose** and **Material 3**. It offers a premium listening experience with deep analytical insights and a beautiful, fluid interface.

### ✨ Key Features
- **Modern Jetpack Compose UI**: A fully declarative UI that is smooth and highly responsive.
- **Deep Analytics**: Track 68 unique listening metrics to understand your habits like never before.
- **Offline-First**: Your data stays on your device. Privacy is built-in.
- **Advanced Metadata**: Lightning-fast scanning and high-quality artwork integration.
- **Customizable**: Themed icons, dynamic colors, and advanced sorting.
