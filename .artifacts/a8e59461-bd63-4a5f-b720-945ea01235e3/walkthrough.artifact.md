# Walkthrough - DHU Launched

The Desktop Head Unit (DHU) has been successfully launched to test the Android Auto implementation.

## Actions Taken
1.  **Port Forwarding**: Established a TCP tunnel on port 5277 to your device (`2f2621ac`).
2.  **DHU Execution**: Launched the DHU binary from your Android SDK extras folder.

## Next Steps
- **Check DHU Window**: You should see a new window on your desktop representing the car display.
- **Interact**: Use your mouse to navigate the Android Auto interface.
- **Troubleshoot**: If the window is blank or doesn't appear, ensure the "Head unit server" is running on your phone and that the device is unlocked.
